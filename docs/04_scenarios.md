# Scenarios

## S1 — Survival

Agents must collect food and avoid hazards.

Observed behaviors:

- exploration
- camping
- risk avoidance

---

## S2 — Tribes With Advantage

One tribe receives a temporary advantage:

- stronger attack
- more speed
- more vision

Observed behaviors:

- territorial control
- raids
- defensive clustering
- avoidance

---

## S3 — Wall Building

Agents can spend energy to create obstacles.

Observed behaviors:

- fortresses
- choke points
- isolation strategies

---

## S4 — Behavioral Phase Changes

Live parameter modification to observe:

- collapse of dominant tribe
- alliance formation
- migration
